About Me

I love rolling up my sleeves and diving into a challenge. Systematically, breaking down a problem with logic and research to produce a solution, learning something new, and applying it immediately. I get a rush from finding solutions, and that's what I love about coding.

Over the past three years, I've worked as a financial advisor with a St. Louis based firm. It was my time here that I learned I could learn something I had no context for previously. The industry was brand new to me, as were the concepts. I quickly researched, learned, applied, and adapted to my new career and industry. My ability to do this with success has bred unmatched confidence in me.

Now I want to take this confidence and marry it with something I'm passionate about, my love for a challenge, and turn that into a rewarding career. I'm an artist at heart, and I love to create. To me, a coder is like an artist. We build a work-of-art for the user experience with eye candy under the hood for a developer to behold. Like film or TV, code is ever-changing, and that's what keeps me engaged.
